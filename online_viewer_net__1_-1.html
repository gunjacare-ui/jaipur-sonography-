About the Centre

Trusted Diagnostics
Since Decades

Jaipur Sonography Centre is one of the most trusted radiology centres in the Rajgarh region of Alwar district, Rajasthan. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable, and compassionate diagnostic imaging services.

Under the expert leadership of Dr. Shailendra Kumar Meena, our centre has grown into a beacon of maternal and general health diagnostics — earning the highest district-level recognition twice in a row.

Advanced Equipment State-of-the-art sonography machines with high-resolution imaging.
100% Safe & Confidential All reports are strictly confidential and follow medical protocols.
Free MAA Scheme Services Completely free ultrasound for eligible pregnant women under the MAA Yojana.
Quick Reporting Prompt, accurate diagnostic reports issued the same day.
